THIS IS A VOICE ASSISTANT MADE IN PYTHON

HOW TO USE IT?
->WHEN STARTING THE PROGRAM, YOU WILL BE ASKED TO CHOOSE YOUR VOICE ASSISTANT
  (JARVIS THE MALE VOICE OR ALEXA THE FEMALE VOICE).
->AFTER THAT A GUI WINDOW WILL APPEAR WITH A BUTTON WHICH YOU CAN CLICK WHENEVER
  YOU WANT TO GIVE VOICE COMMANDS.
->YOU CAN SAY THINGS LIKE, TELL DATE, TELL NEWS, TELL WEATHER, OPEN (CAMERA
  ,CALCULATOR,COMMANDPROMPT,SETTINGS) ETC IN YOUR COMPUTER.
->YOU CAN ALSO OPEN DIFFERENT APPLICATIONS INSTALLED IN YOUR COMPUTER,BY ADDING
   THE DIRECTORIES IN STARTFILE FUNCTION.
